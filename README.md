# WastinaSafety 

## Planned Mobile App Project

**WastinaSafety** is a proposed mobile application designed to improve workplace safety by replacing unreliable paper-based tracking systems with a digital audit trail for personal protective equipment (PPE) and safety gear.

The goal of the project is to help organizations prevent workers from using **damaged**, **expired**, or **unverified** safety equipment.  
By digitizing PPE tracking and inspection records, the system aims to improve safety compliance and reduce workplace accidents in high-risk environments.

>  **Project Status:** This project is currently in the **planning and design phase**. Development has **not started yet**.

##  Project Objective

The main objective of **WastinaSafety** is to design and develop a mobile-based solution that can digitally manage workplace safety equipment records and reduce the risks associated with manual paper-based systems.

This project aims to:

- Replace manual PPE tracking with a digital system
- Prevent the use of damaged or expired safety gear
- Improve inspection transparency and accountability
- Create a reliable audit trail for safety compliance
- Support safer work environments in high-risk industries

---

##  Problem Statement

Many workplaces still rely on **paper-based methods** to track personal protective equipment (PPE) such as helmets, gloves, boots, goggles, and safety vests.

This creates several challenges:

- Records may be lost or damaged
- Equipment condition is difficult to monitor consistently
- Expired safety gear may still be assigned to workers
- Manual verification is slow and unreliable
- Safety audits become difficult to manage
- Workplace accidents may increase due to poor tracking

Because of these limitations, there is a need for a **digital mobile solution** that makes safety equipment management more accurate, accessible, and reliable.

---

## Proposed Solution

**WastinaSafety** is planned as a mobile application that will allow safety officers, supervisors, and administrators to digitally track PPE and safety equipment.

The proposed system will help users:

- Register safety equipment
- Track condition and expiry dates
- Assign PPE to workers
- Record inspections and maintenance history
- Receive alerts for damaged or expired equipment
- Maintain a digital audit trail for compliance and reporting

---

##  Planned Features

The following features are proposed for the first version of the app:

- 👤**User Authentication**
  - Secure login for admins, supervisors, and workers
-  **PPE Registration**
  - Add and manage safety equipment records

-  **Worker Assignment**
  - Assign equipment to individual workers

-  **Expiry Date Tracking**
  - Monitor expiration dates of safety gear

-  **Condition Monitoring**
  - Mark items as active, damaged, expired, or replaced

- **Inspection Records**
  - Log inspection and maintenance history

-  **Alerts & Notifications**
  - Notify responsible staff when equipment becomes unsafe

-  **Audit Trail**
  - Maintain a history of actions for compliance review

## 🔄 Proposed Workflow

1. Admin registers safety equipment in the system  
2. Safety officer assigns PPE to a worker  
3. The app stores issue date, expiry date, and condition  
4. Inspection records are updated regularly  
5. Damaged or expired equipment is flagged  
6. Supervisors review records and take action  
7. Audit logs support compliance verification
